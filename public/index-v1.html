Our Story

Crafting Unforgettable
Culinary Experiences

Elamrit was born from a belief that food is more than sustenance — it is ceremony. Every plate we present carries the weight of occasion, the warmth of tradition, and the precision of craft. From intimate private dinners to grand destination weddings spanning across India, we bring the same obsessive attention to flavour, presentation, and service.

Rooted in Pune and catering Pan India, Elamrit blends the richness of Indian culinary heritage with a modern, elevated aesthetic — creating experiences that linger long after the last bite.
