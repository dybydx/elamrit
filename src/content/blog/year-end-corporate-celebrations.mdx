Year-end events often mix generations and reporting lines — which means alcohol pacing, vegetarian ratios, and dessert timing matter as much as the centrepiece. We help you decide where to invest (usually proteins and service) and where restraint reads as confidence (usually gimmick courses).

Budget clarity up front saves awkward substitutions later. We are comfortable working to a per-head band if you share expected attendance bands and any union or venue constraints early.

## Kids, plus-ones, and awards runs

If children are invited, we plan for earlier eating windows and finger-friendly options without turning the ballroom into a birthday party. If awards overrun, holding strategies for mains become part of the run-of-show — not a panicked call from the kitchen.
