Launches often compress eating into awkward gaps: registration crushes, stage runs long, and suddenly everyone is hungry during the one moment you need silence. We align bites with your run-of-show — standing-friendly first, seated or substantial only when the agenda allows.

Branding on F&B should be tasteful — literally. Coloured macarons can work; gimmicks that fight your product story rarely do.

## Press and VIP lanes

Separate holding for media and executives is common. We keep dietary and timing notes consistent so nobody discovers an allergen gap because “the green room had a different menu.”
