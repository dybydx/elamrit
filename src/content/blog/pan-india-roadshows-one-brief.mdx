Roadshows test communication. A single playbook for dietary codes, brand colours on buffets where relevant, and named escalation contacts beats reinventing the wheel in each city. We document what worked in Pune so Hyderabad inherits the lesson, not the guesswork.

Local preferences still matter — spice calibration, breakfast habits, regional sweets — within guardrails you approve once. The goal is recognisable quality, not identical ingredients that fight the local palate.

## Load-in and venue variance

Ballrooms and convention halls are not interchangeable. We build contingency into timelines when lift access, dock distances, or power for live stations differ. Your attendees should not feel that variance — only the host team should know what we adapted.
