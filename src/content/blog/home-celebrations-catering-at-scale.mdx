Private homes offer warmth — and constraints: narrow gates, single-phase power, neighbours who did not RSVP. We survey load-in honestly and design menus that fit the kitchen you have, not the one we wish you had.

Buffet lines in gardens need lighting, flooring, and rain thinking. We coordinate with your security and society rules early so the team is not arguing at the gate in brocade.

## Noise, parking, and wrap

Residents remember how you leave as much as how you arrive. Clear wrap times and waste handling are part of our plan — not an afterthought when the last guest leaves.
